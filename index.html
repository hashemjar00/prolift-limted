About Us

ProLift Limited is a leading provider of elevator installation, maintenance, and modernization services. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, we are committed to delivering innovative solutions that elevate spaces and enhance lives.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians are dedicated to excellence, ensuring that every project is completed with the highest level of quality and professionalism.

Our Services

Elevator Installation

We specialize in the installation of a wide range of elevator types, including passenger elevators, freight elevators, panoramic elevators, and more. Our expert team handles every aspect of the installation process, from planning and design to final inspection.

Elevator Maintenance

Our comprehensive maintenance plans ensure that your elevators operate smoothly and efficiently, reducing downtime and minimizing the risk of unexpected breakdowns. We offer regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and 24/7 emergency support.
